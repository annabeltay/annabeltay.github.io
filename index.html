Labgoo Internship

Aug 2016 - Jun 2017

During this internship, I was primarily on the iOS team where I worked with Swift, Objective C and Xcode to build features and fix bugs for the Wondermall iOS app. I also had the chance to learn server development where I used different cloud stacks such as Google App Engine, Google Compute Engine and Amazon Web Services. NUS Overseas Colleges

Aug 2016 - Jun 2017

The NUS Overseas Colleges (NOC) Programme is an internship programme with a strong emphasis on technology entrepreneurship. During the one-year NOC SV programme, I was a full time intern at Labgoo and a part-time student who took both Stanford and NUS modules.

Through Stanford's Entrepreneur Thought Leaders module, I interacted with and learned from professionals involved in the vibrant start-up scene in the valley. My entrepreneurial experience was also enhanced by an intensive NUS course on Lean Startup methodology where I used the Business Model Canvas and the Value Proposition Canvas to validate possible ideas for a startup.

Stanford CS155

Computer and Network Security

Stanford's CS155 is an intensive and fun module where I learned and implemented common security exploits as well as their respective defenses for the module's assignments. Some of the exploits include control hijacking attacks, CSRF and XSS. Additionally, I also learned the basics of network security such as port scanning, wireshark packet sniffing and packet log processing.
